Welcome to the S.E.P.I.A. app for browser, iOS and Android! :-) In this little tutorial you will learn the basics to get started with your personal assistant.

Here is an overview of the controls:
Some buttons have a press-and-hold (long-press) action assigned as well like the shortcuts (settings) and the mic (audio-input reset).

If you want to ask S.E.P.I.A. a question just tap the mic or type in the chat field, send the message and wait for your answer. On rare occasions your mic might get stuck in "gray"-mode. If that happens you can release it with a long-press on the mic itself.

When you start S.E.P.I.A. you usually end up in your personal start-screen a.k.a 'my view'. This view will show you personal info like upcoming reminders, custom buttons or recommendations based on the time of day. You can fill this screen with results you get by chatting with S.E.P.I.A. (more about this later).

There are 2 more views to interact with S.E.P.I.A., the chat view and the results view. You can reach them using the controls on the top or by swiping from the edges of the screen (similar to iOS navigation).

Tutorial - Page 4 of 5

When you ask S.E.P.I.A. to add stuff to your to-do or shopping lists you will see a card like this:



Depending on the list type each item can have 2-3 different states. For to-do-lists tap the check-button to switch between open (no color), in-progress (yellow) and done (green).

Items in your list can be moved by activating the drag & drop mode. To do this press-and-hold the check-button. The list item will quickly flash and become grayish. Release the check-button and then use it again to drag the item around, drop it at its new position or even move it to a different list. After 3s of inactivity the drag & drop mode will automatically switch off.

Modifications to the list are not saved automatically and you can undo them simply by reloading the list. If changes are detected the save-button will appear in the upper-left corner. Press it to make all changes permanent.

Tutorial - Page 5 of 5

There are many more things to discover in S.E.P.I.A. like chatting with users (group or private), playing radio music, teaching your own commands, using gamepads to trigger actions or changing skins (the look) so be sure to check out all the menus and play around with all the buttons :-). A good place to start is the shortcuts menu on the bottom-left (please note that this menu might not fully work in demo-mode yet).

Always-On mode:

The always-on mode can be activated via the shortcuts menu or by a double-tap on the back-button (Android only). It is a special mode that shows only a very minimalistic interface and prevents the screen from turning off on mobile devices so that S.E.P.I.A. can keep running like a smart-speaker.
